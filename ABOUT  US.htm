ABOUT  US

Manviya Vaibhav Vikash Nyas a charitable trust founded in year 1986 has consistently displayed a complete awareness in the field of education by setting up the English medium Senior Secondary School for boys in Patna by the name of Gyan Niketan in 1986. Its first batch appeared in 1990 and till now a total of 19 batches have appeared in the Central Board of Secondary Education. AISSE. School got upgraded to Senior Secondary level after producing excellent results in the year 1999. After several years of assiduous endeavour, the M.V.V. Nyas is now in a position to declare with conviction that Gyan Niketan is an excellent result oriented human resources, development school of National repute. It is a school with a difference. It has hostel facilities from Nursery to Std. XII but with a limited capacity. It has a spacious playground and recently an extra two acres of land has been added to the existing playground.

 

 

The school at present has two sites. Classes for Nursery to Std. IV are held on the Bihar Vidyapeeth Campus, Near Sadaquat Ashram and the senior Wing from Std. V to Std. XII runs at the new site Vitthal Vihar Complex of West Bailey Road, Gola Road, near the canal. Both the schools have options for students to lead the hostel life but in a limited capacity.

Gyan Niketan provides an environment for all round development of pupils and their integration with the society based on values of Indian culture. It tries to inculcate in the pupils values of personal hygiene, correct uniform cleanliness of the surroundings, obedience to their elders and, above all, discipline. To achieve all these, along with a high academic standard, a highly motivated and dedicated team qualified teachers are playing a very important role to help in the growth of their students (Gyanis).

 

The rapport between the teachers and the students also help the pupils in preparing their lessons and to use new methods of teaching techniques viz. demonstration, experimentation, project work and home assignments to make their lessons interesting which leads to good results.

 

Gyan Niketan has a glorious past. Many eminent educationists are attached to this institution. Their regular visits to the school and their advice help the institution to grow effectively in all direction.

 

The school was established in 1986. Today it is considered as on of the finest educational institutions in the city and around. It has maintained a high standard of education and its students have brought laurels to the school year after year.

 

The school management, consisting of retired personalities from the field of education, administration, and legal luminaries give their best to provide a pragmatic, child centered education, to help a Gyani to become “a perfect man”.

 

The School curriculum is related to our environment that reflects our culture, while remaining, at the same time, abreast with modern development.

 

The School is recognized by C.B.S.E. up to class – XII and has more than 2400 students on its rolls. The school has its sprawling building and well equipped science and computer laboratories that are regularly upgraded and additional library-cum-reading room and an audio-visual room and some additional class rooms have been recently added on the third floor. The School is soon going to construct an auditorium and would accommodate about 1000 pupils at a time.

 

The main aim of this school is to enhance mental, physical and spiritual growth of its students (Gyanis) by maintaining a balance between sports, academics and co-curricular activities.

 

The school believes in quality in not quantity. Thus it has no branch in the city and around. However we are in process of starting a girls school with the same values in Digha very soon.

 

 

 

The School is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi up to the Senior Secondary Level. The Affiliation No. is 330010.
